About company

Visaje Clair is a newly established beauty cream company based in Kashmir, Pakistan. Despite being new to the market, the company has quickly gained a reputation for producing high-quality, effective skincare products that are tailored to the needs of the local population. Visaje Clair's products are formulated using only the finest, most natural ingredients, which are carefully selected to ensure that they are gentle on the skin yet highly effective. The company's team of expert researchers and chemists work tirelessly to develop formulas that are tailored to the specific needs of Pakistani women and Men, taking into account factors such as climate, lifestyle, and cultural practices.
